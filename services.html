Fresh Surveys

Fresh Surveys involve determining the boundaries of a parcel of land that has never been surveyed before.

Subdivisions & Amalgamations

Subdivision is the process of dividing a large parcel of land into smaller, individual lots. Amalgamation, on the other hand, is the opposite process, where two or more adjacent parcels of land are combined into a single, larger parcel.

Boundary Openings

Boundary Opening is the process of determining the actual boundaries of a plot of land which already has a certificate of tilte.

Land Title Processing

Land title processing is the series of steps involved in verifying, documenting, and officially registering ownership of a piece of land.

Land Title Transfers

This refers to the legal process of transferring ownership of a piece of land from one person or entity to another. This process typically involves documenting the sale or transfer, updating the official land records, and issuing a new land title in the name of the new owner.

Engineering Surveys

Engineering surveying is the practice of determining the accurate position and dimensions of natural and man-made features on or beneath the Earth's surface to support the planning, design, and construction of engineering projects.
